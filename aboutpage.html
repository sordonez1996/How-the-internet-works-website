About Page

This website is an assignment for a graduate level course, IS 601 - Web Systems Development. This course is offered at New Jersey Institute of Technology in Newark New Jersey.

The purpose of this website is to gain a better understanding of the history of the internet. Sections of the website include Domain names/ICANN, IP Addresses, Packets/Routing, DNS, HTTP/HTTPS protocols, and W3C/HTML/CSS. These sections are based of from videos and the powerpoint slides given to us from the assignment. We hope you find this useful in trying to get a better understanding of How The Internet Works.
